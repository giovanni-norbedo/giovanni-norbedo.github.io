About

I'm a student of Artificial Intelligence and Data Analytics at the University of Trieste, finishing my bachelor's and moving toward a master's degree. I'm fascinated by mathematics and love breaking down complex ideas into clear, visual forms.

I've been programming since I was young, driven by curiosity and the joy of building things. I enjoy working with Python, Django, React, and Flutter, and I'm drawn to clean architectures, best practices, and code that feels both simple and elegant.

Outside of coding, I like reading, exploring old electronics, and listening to music on my vinyls. I keep active with gym and basketball, enjoy visiting art exhibitions, and relaxing by the sea. I also challenge myself regularly with math, programming, and hacking problems to keep my skills sharp.
